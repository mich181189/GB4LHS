GB4LHS - London Hackspace Open Day Special Event Station

26th May 2019

After many months of work, London Hackspace is finally rebuilt after moving to its new space in Wembley, North London. In order to celebrate this, and show the space off to potential new members, an open day is being held on Sunday the 26th May 2019. As part of this, the radio subgroup is active throughout the day as GB4LHS.

We're running a Yaesu FT-991A (owned by Michael, M0ISR) into a fan dipole made with laser cut seperators.
